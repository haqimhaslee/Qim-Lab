Workshop & Training

Web App Cloud AI

Our workshops empower individuals and teams with practical, in-demand tech skills. Led by industry experts, our hands-on training covers key areas like mobile development, web technologies, and AI, providing the knowledge you need to stay ahead.

We believe in empowering individuals and teams with the in-demand skills needed to thrive in the digital landscape. Our hands-on workshops and focused training sessions are designed to provide practical knowledge and expertise in today's most critical technologies. Taught by our seasoned industry professionals, our courses are tailored to be engaging, informative, and directly applicable to real-world projects.

Whether you're a beginner looking to start a new career path or a seasoned professional aiming to upskill, our programs offer a clear pathway to success.

Web Development

Comprehensive workshop that takes you from the basics to advanced web technologies. Learn to build modern, responsive, and dynamic websites using industry-standard tools and frameworks.

Cloud Services 101

Understand the power and potential of cloud computing. This workshop covers the essential concepts and services of major cloud platforms, helping you leverage the cloud for scalable, secure, and efficient infrastructure.

Flutter 101

Dive into the world of cross-platform mobile development with Flutter, Google's UI toolkit for building beautiful, natively compiled applications for mobile, web, and desktop from a single codebase

Gen AI 101

Foundational concepts of Generative AI, different types of models (e.g., LLMs, diffusion models), practical applications for content and data creation, and an introduction to the ethical considerations of using this technology.

Gen AI Suite

Core AI concepts and prompt engineering, how to effectively use Gemini for advanced conversational tasks, harnessing the power of NotebookLM for research and data analysis, and developing custom AI applications with Google AI Studio.

Power Platform (Coming Soon)

Empower yourself and your team to build custom business solutions without extensive coding knowledge. The Microsoft Power Platform is a suite of low-code tools that enables you to create powerful apps, automate workflows, and analyze data to drive business efficiency.
