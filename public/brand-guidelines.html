Brand Guidelines

Introduction

Our Brand Essence

Qim Lab’s brand is a reflection of innovation, professionalism, and creativity. These guidelines ensure our visual identity remains consistent across all platforms.

Logo Usage

Always use the official Qim Lab logo in its original proportions and colors. Maintain a clear space around the logo equal to the height of the letter “Q” in Qim Lab.

  • ✅ Use only approved logo versions (full color, monochrome, inverted)
  • ❌ Do not stretch, distort, or recolor the logo
  • ❌ Do not place logo on complex backgrounds

Color Palette

Our brand colors convey trust, creativity, and professionalism. Use them consistently in all marketing materials.

Orange #FF8000
Dark Gray #2C2C2C
White #FFFFFF
Black #000000

Typography

Our typography reinforces our clean and modern identity.

  • Headings: Raleway, Bold
  • Body Text: Nunito Sans, Regular
  • Special Text: Roboto, Medium

Tone & Voice

Qim Lab communicates with a tone that is:

  • Professional yet approachable
  • Innovative and forward-thinking
  • Clear, concise, and engaging

Do's & Don'ts

✅ Do:
  • Use official assets from the Qim Lab brand kit
  • Maintain consistency in fonts and colors
  • Ensure readability across all media
❌ Don’t:
  • Alter or distort the logo
  • Use unapproved color variations
  • Mix unrelated design elements
